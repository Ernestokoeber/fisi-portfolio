Frequently Asked Questions for Training Companies

In what framework is an assignment possible?

The assignment takes place within several clearly defined practical blocks during the retraining to become a specialist for system integration. The timeframes are long-term planable and can be coordinated early on.

In which areas can I support You?

Support in day-to-day IT operations, including system maintenance, troubleshooting, documentation, network and server tasks, automation via scripting, as well as rollouts and testing.

How high is the supervision effort?

New technologies and internal processes I work through systematically and independently. Questions are asked specifically and solution-oriented, to keep the supervision effort low.

How is the compensation regulated?

The practical phases take place as part of the retraining program. The compensation is paid by the responsible educational institution. No personnel costs arise for the practice company.

Is there an obligation to offer permanent employment?

No. There is no obligation to offer permanent employment. The focus lies on a professionally meaningful collaboration during the practical phases.
